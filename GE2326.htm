Mosaics Matrix Casino Game!

Rule of the game

Given a 3*3 Matrix, in which (0,0), (0,2), (1,1), (2,0), (2,2) are painted black, and the left 4 blocks remain white.(As the image shown below)
Now given two dices (represented as two numbers). Everytime you click the "ROLL" butten, it will randomly display two independent numbers, range from 1-6.
The two numbers are regarded as the result of the dice. And these two numbers will define a coordinate on the matrix.
In each round of the game, you are expected to row the two dices two times and get two coordinates on the matrix and record their colors (black/white).
If the two colors are the same, then you win 150 HKD. Else, you lose 150 HKD.
